Grandma's Chocolate Cake

A delicious homemade desert!

This chocolate cake has been in our family for generations.

Ingredients :

  • 2 Cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup choco powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 1800C
  2. Mix all dry Ingredients

Cooking Info:

Prep. Time Cook TIme Servings Difficulty
20min 20min 1 Easy

Chef's Tips:

Tip 1: Use high-quality choco powder.

Tip 2: Let the cake cool before eating.
